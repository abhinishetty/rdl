AI Based PPE Monitoring System

RDL AI-Based PPE Monitoring System is an intelligent computer-vision solution designed to automatically detect personal protective equipment compliance across industrial environments in real time.

By leveraging AI, edge computing, and existing CCTV infrastructure, the system minimizes workplace risks, prevents accidents, and ensures regulatory compliance without manual supervision.

What is it?

An AI-based PPE Monitoring System uses computer vision and machine learning to detect whether workers are wearing mandatory safety gear such as

  • Helmets
  • Gloves
  • Safety Boots
  • Face Masks
  • Reflective Vests
  • Goggles

In real time. At all critical points. Without human supervision.

Ideal For
  • Manufacturing Plants
  • Construction Sites
  • Mining Operations
  • Compliance Rate by Department.
  • Predictive Risk Analytics.
  • Chemical & Pharma Industries.
  • Violation Logs with Timestamp & Person.
    • Exportable Reports for Safety Audits.
How It Works

CCTV / Surveillance Feeds

Monitored by AI model trained for PPE detection

Worker Detection

Uses facial recognition or RFID / QR codes

PPE Compliance Check

Real-time analysis of required PPE

Alerts & Reporting

Audio / visual alerts with automatic reports
