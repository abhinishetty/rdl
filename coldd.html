Cold Storage Monitoring Solution

An IoT-based cold storage monitoring solution enables continuous, real-time tracking of refrigerator and freezer temperatures, automated compliance reporting, and condition-based alerts.

Designed to support storage durations ranging from days to years, the system ensures regulatory adherence, preserves product integrity, reduces spoilage risks, and enhances energy efficiency through data-driven insights.

Benefits of implementing IoT solutions for cold storage facilities

  • Real-time monitoring
  • Employee and asset safety
  • Increased profitability
  • Reduced spoilage
  • Improved energy efficiency

Applications

  • Agriculture industry
  • Blood banks
  • Food & beverage industry
  • Healthcare industry
  • Pharmaceutical facilities
  • Restaurant chains
  • Food manufacturing facilities

Cold Storage Data Logger – WiFi / GPRS / 4G / LTE

Cold Storage Data Logger comes with IP68 enclosure and supports a variety of I/O interfaces including Analog, Digital, MODBUS RTU and MODBUS TCP with multiple temperature sensor interfacing options.

This data logger can be used in cold storage monitoring systems, food storage and food processing environments.

Designed to seamlessly integrate with IoT and analytical processing systems, the device supports SSL with configurable protocols such as JSON, MQTT and FTP.
