EMS – ONLINE LIVE ESD MONITORING SYSTEM

100% Live ESD Compliance. Zero Compromise

RDL EMS Online Live ESD Monitoring Solution continuously monitors operator wrist-strap resistance and verifies grounding integrity of ESD-protected work surfaces in real time. The system ensures uninterrupted monitoring of personnel and workstations, enabling proactive ESD fault detection and stable electronics manufacturing.

Features

  • Alerting with fault location
  • Accurate earth resistance measurement
  • Bonding & grounding connectivity status
  • Electrical grounding history with fault prediction
  • Automatic alternate ground source selection
  • Grounding system maintenance alert
  • MODBUS TCP / RTU, JSON, MQTT, FTP support
  • Real-time network status availability
  • Visual & audio failure alerts
  • Modular design & extendibility
  • Easy configuration & maintenance
  • Centralized SQL data storage

Why Live ESD Monitoring System is Required?

Equipment, furniture, clothing and common workplace items can store thousands of volts of electrostatic charge, while only 25 volts can permanently damage an IC.

ESD losses globally range between $0.5–5 billion annually due to unseen damage in SMT manufacturing.

ESD & Semiconductor Devices

Electrostatic discharge is a sudden surge of electricity between two charged objects. Repeated discharge events degrade internal IC structures over time.

Currents passing through PN junctions burn microscopic holes and cause permanent device failures.

ANSI/ESD S20.20 Control Program

ANSI/ESD S20.20 is the global standard ensuring protection from electrostatic charges above 100V.

  • Reduces component failures
  • Saves cost & increases quality
  • Improves industry compliance
