UHF RFID Readers

RDL UHF reader device is enabled with the world’s best high performance chip and higher sensitivity antenna to continuously monitor, track and identify items across large areas.

IP65 rated UHF readers are widely used in production automation, parking automation, fixed asset management, hospitals, logistics, warehouse inventory and access control.

Features

  • Software SDK / API for faster application development
  • Ethernet, WiFi, 4G/LTE, RS232, RS485, Wiegand support
  • TCP/IP, HTTP & MQTT protocol support
  • Wide area coverage over wired & wireless networks
  • Aluminum casting with IP65 rated housing
  • Integrated reader and antenna design
  • SDKs: .NET, Java, Python, Android
  • Handheld, wall-mount & desktop variants
  • Reading range: 50 cm to 15 meters
  • Frequency: 902–926 MHz / 865–868 MHz
  • Read rate: 200 tags per second
  • RF Power up to 30 dBm
