Utilities Monitoring System

On-line utilities monitoring

Utilities Monitoring System is the best automation system for collecting, monitoring, and optimizing energy, water, gas, and compressed air data to boost utilization and reliability while lowering costs.

It quickly identifies and eliminates areas of waste, resulting in significant cost savings within weeks of installation.

Benefits of Implementing Utilities Monitoring System

  • Real-time end-to-end traceability of consumption
  • Precise identification and cost analysis of utilities
  • Centralized monitoring with automatic pre-warning alarms
  • Baseline tracking to avoid penalty costs
  • Proactive alerts for failures and disturbances
  • KPI-based dashboards for utility goal setting
  • Reduced production cost and improved energy efficiency

Applications

  • Manufacturing Industry
  • Food & Beverage Industry
  • Healthcare
  • Pharmaceutical Facilities
  • Restaurants
  • Institutions
  • Data Centers

Utilities Meters

Utilities Monitoring System supports all kinds of utilities meters and enables remote data acquisition for energy, water, gas, and compressed air.
