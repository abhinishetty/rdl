Andon – Production Monitoring System

RDL Industrial Andon Solution is a smart, real-time production monitoring and alerting system designed to improve shop-floor visibility, reduce downtime, and enable faster decision-making.

By combining visual indicators, audio alerts, and digital dashboards, the solution empowers operators, supervisors, and management to respond instantly to production issues.

Key Challenges Addressed

  • Unplanned machine downtime
  • Delayed response to production issues
  • Lack of real-time shop-floor visibility
  • Manual escalation and communication gaps
  • Inefficient productivity & loss tracking

Solution Highlights

  • Real-time visual & audio alerts
  • Multi-level escalation to supervisors & managers
  • Live digital dashboards
  • PLC, SCADA, MES & ERP integration
  • Historical analytics & reports
  • Customizable Andon dashboards
