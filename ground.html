Why Bonding & Grounding Need to be Monitored?

Static Electricity

Static electricity results from the interaction of dissimilar materials. It may occur during liquid transfer, mixing, agitation or friction.

Electrostatic discharge can ignite flammable vapors causing explosions. The two most important ways to prevent static sparks are bonding and grounding.

Bonding & Grounding

Bonding eliminates electrical potential difference between objects. Grounding provides a safe conductive path to earth.

Why is it important to bond and ground containers?

Bonding prevents static spark formation during liquid transfer between metal containers.

Bonding and grounding are essential in drums, mixers, dip tanks and flammable liquid handling.

Static Build-up

Insulating materials like glass, polymers and Teflon can accumulate high static charge.

Electrostatic attraction increases contamination risks in healthcare environments.
