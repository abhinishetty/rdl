Shop Floor Digitization

The Challenge
  • Planning and scheduling were time-consuming and person dependent
  • Handling physical job cards, operator performance and quality sheets was difficult
  • Improving throughput, quality and machine uptime was a challenge
  • Optimizing scrap and utilities consumption
Benefits
  • Increased OEE up to 30%
  • Quality increased up to 15%
  • Energy consumption reduced by 8%
  • Energy bill reduced by 10%
  • 25 manpower saved
  • Scrap reduced by 50%
  • Throughput increased by 15%
  • Paperless shop floor – Go Green initiative
