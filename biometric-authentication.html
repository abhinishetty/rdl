Biometric Authentication System

Biometric Authentication System is designed to enhance the security and access control of PLC (Programmable Logic Controller) and HMI (Human Machine Interface) systems. It ensures that only authorized personnel can access critical industrial control systems. The product is tested with all PLCs and supports multiple interface options.

Features

  • Fingerprint, facial & iris authentication
  • Seamless PLC & HMI integration
  • Encrypted biometric data storage
  • User & access control management
  • Real-time access monitoring
  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Fingerprint enrollment: 1,000 users
  • Memory: 16 GB
  • RS485, MODBUS RTU/TCP, RS232, USB
  • Power supply: DC 24V

Benefits

  • Higher security for PLC & HMI systems
  • Prevents unauthorized access
  • Reduces risk of cyber threats
  • Compliance with industrial standards
  • Eliminates password vulnerabilities
  • Centralized user administration
  • Improved operational safety

Applications

  • Manufacturing automation lines
  • Energy & utility systems
  • Water & wastewater treatment
  • Transportation & logistics
  • Oil & gas refineries
  • Petrochemical plants
  • 4M (Man, Machine, Material, Method)
