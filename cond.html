Remote Condition Monitoring System

Condition Monitoring – Increased Reliability

Remote condition monitoring solution ideal for motors that play a critical role in production and sensitive applications. Operators can remotely monitor health, performance and efficiency of motors, detect early signs of potential problems and schedule maintenance to avoid unplanned downtime.

Advantages of Condition Monitoring

  • Proactive alerts of potential problems reduce downtime
  • Minimize unplanned downtime and maintenance cost
  • Condition-based maintenance improves reliability and uptime
  • Early detection improves safety and motor lifespan
  • Remote & real-time monitoring increases operational efficiency

What is Condition Monitoring?

Machines and systems are carefully observed for changes that could indicate upcoming failure. Sensor data is analyzed to detect changes in parameters such as temperature, vibration, speed, energy consumption, suction and discharge pressure during operation.
