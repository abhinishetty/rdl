Vibration Sensor

Advanced Vibration Sensor is a high-performance device designed to accurately monitor and analyze vibrations in various industrial and commercial applications. With its advanced features and capabilities, this sensor provides valuable insights into equipment health, allowing for preventive maintenance and improved operational efficiency.

Advantages

  • Proactive alerts of potential problems reduce downtime.
  • Minimizes unplanned downtime and reduces maintenance cost and time.
  • Condition-based maintenance increases reliability and uptime.
  • Early detection improves safety and extends motor lifespan.
  • Remote and real-time monitoring enables higher operational efficiency.

Specification

  • Sensor: 3-axial MEMS accelerometer
  • Velocity Range: 0–25 mm/sec ±10%
  • Output: 4–20mA / Modbus RTU / Wi-Fi / Bluetooth
  • Shock Survival: 10,000g
  • Sensitivity: Less than 5%
  • Settling Time: 3 seconds

Applications

Our Advanced Vibration Sensor can be used across a wide range of industrial applications, including:

  • Manufacturing Plants
  • Oil & Gas Refineries
  • Power Generation Facilities
  • Mining Operations
  • Automotive Assembly Lines
  • HVAC Systems
  • Wind Turbines
  • Gear Boxes & Pumps
  • Fans & Motors
  • Compressors & Centrifuges
  • Conveyors, Handlers & Rolls
  • Dryers, Presses & Spindles
  • Tooling & Process Equipment

Overall, the Advanced Vibration Sensor delivers accurate and reliable vibration monitoring, enabling proactive maintenance, reduced downtime, and improved productivity.
